HOW TO MINE

Registration

Connect your wallet on the website. After connecting, you will receive a unique mining wallet address.

Deposit SOL

Send SOL to your mining wallet. These funds are used to purchase Hash Power.

Purchase Hash Power

  • 0.1 SOL = 100 Hash Power.
  • The more Hash Power you have, the greater your mining share.
  • Minimum deposit: 0.105 SOL.
  • Transaction fee: 0.005 SOL.
  • Hash Power is valid for 24 hours, after which it resets, and you can purchase new Hash Power.The limited duration of Hash Power allows for better regulation of the game's economy. This prevents situations where players with significant power dominate indefinitely, reducing opportunities for newcomers.

Mining Process

  • The game mines 1,200 blocks daily.
  • Block processing time: 1-2 minutes.
  • Each block distributes CDUST tokens among miners proportionally to their Hash Power.

Claiming Rewards

  • Collect accumulated tokens every 15 minutes.
  • Tokens are sent to your designated withdrawal address.

Upgrade Strategy

Increase your Hash Power by depositing SOL daily to gain a larger share of rewards.

Withdrawals

Withdrawals are available at any time.

Processing time: 1 to 5 minutes.

Note

SOL used to purchase Hash Power is non-refundable and supports the mining ecosystem.
