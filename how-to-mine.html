How to Start Mining

Registration

Visit our website and connect your crypto wallet. Once connected, you will receive a unique mining wallet address.

Purchase HP Token

  • Buy the HP token on Pump.fun through our website.
  • ⚠️ Important: Only tokens purchased through our site can be used for mining.

Send HP for Mining

  • Send HP to your assigned mining wallet address.
  • HP will be burned, which automatically starts the mining process.

Start Mining

Once HP is sent, mining starts automatically, and you begin earning CDUST.

Claiming Rewards

  • CDUST is credited every hour.
  • Tokens are automatically sent to your wallet.

Upgrade Strategy

  • To continue mining, send new HP every 24 hours.
  • In the future, other tokens can be used for mining instead of HP.
