HOW IT WORKS

Registration

Connect your crypto wallet on our website. Once connected, you will receive a unique mining address.

Buying Hash Power

HP is the first available token for starting CDUST mining. To start mining, follow these steps:

  • Buy HP through our website.
  • Send it to your assigned mining address.
  • Once sent, HP is burned, and your mining starts automatically.

How Does HP Affect Mining?

  • The more HP you send, the more CDUST you will earn.
  • Minimum required deposit: 1000 HP.
  • Burning HP increases the value of the remaining HP, making mining more rewarding for all users.

Mining Process

  • The game mines 1,200 blocks daily.
  • CDUST is distributed proportionally among miners based on the amount of burned HP.
  • Mining is active for 24 hours, after which you must buy HP again.

Claiming Rewards

  • CDUST rewards are credited every hour.
  • Tokens are automatically sent to your wallet.

How to Continue Mining?

  • Mining stops after 24 hours.
  • To continue, buy HP again and send it to the system.
  • In the future, you will be able to use other tokens instead of HP for mining.

Tokenomics (Token Distribution)

  • Total CDUST Supply: 1,000,000,000
  • Mining (rewards): 750,000,000 (75%)
  • Liquidity (exchange trading): 150,000,000 (15%)
  • Community & Marketing: 100,000,000 (10%)

Withdrawals

  • Withdrawals are available anytime.
  • Processing time: 1-5 minutes.
