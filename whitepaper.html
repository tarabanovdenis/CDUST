Whitepaper

Abstract

CDUST is an innovative Web3 mining game built on the Solana blockchain. It combines virtual cryptocurrency mining with engaging gameplay mechanics. This document outlines the CDUST system, its tokenomics, and the game mechanics that create a unique, sustainable, and potentially rewarding experience for users in the cryptocurrency space.

1. Introduction

The rise of blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies has opened new opportunities for gamification in the financial sector. CDUST leverages this potential by creating a virtual mining ecosystem on the Solana blockchain. Users can participate in cryptocurrency mining directly through a website, without the need for physical mining hardware.

Registration and Hash Power

Users join the CDUST ecosystem by registering through the official website. After registration, the system generates a unique mining wallet for each user. The core resource for mining is Hash Power, which is acquired by depositing SOL under the following terms:

  • 0.1 SOL = 100 Hash Power
  • Minimum deposit: 0.105 SOL (including a 0.005 SOL transaction fee)
  • Hash Power is valid for 24 hours and must be renewed daily.

Block Generation and Mining

CDUST maintains a stable generation of 1,200 blocks daily.

  • Block processing time is 1-2 minutes, ensuring a steady mining pace.
  • Each mined block distributes CDUST tokens to active miners proportionally to their Hash Power share in the network.

Reward Distribution and Claiming

Accumulated CDUST tokens can be claimed every 15 minutes.

Tokens are automatically sent to the withdrawal address specified by the user.

Total Supply and Distribution

The CDUST ecosystem has a fixed total supply of 1,000,000,000 CDUST, distributed as follows:

  • 750,000,000 CDUST (75%) — allocated for mining rewards
  • 150,000,000 CDUST (15%) — allocated for DEX and CEX liquidity
  • 100,000,000 CDUST (10%) — allocated for community and marketing rewards

Risks and Disclaimer

  • CDUST is a blockchain-based mining game, designed for entertainment and engagement in the Web3 space.
  • The CDUST token is not an investment asset and should not be purchased with expectations of financial returns.
  • The cryptocurrency market is highly volatile, and participants risk losing their entire contributions.
  • The game mechanics and tokenomics described in this document are experimental and subject to change.
  • Participation in CDUST should be limited to funds users can afford to lose.
  • The project may face risks such as hacks, economic changes, or unforeseen challenges.
  • This document does not constitute financial, investment, or trading advice.

CDUST is a Web3 game designed for fun and to explore blockchain mechanics. If you are seeking investment opportunities, consult a financial expert.

Conclusion

CDUST introduces a novel approach to virtual mining, leveraging the Solana blockchain to create an engaging and profitable experience. By integrating Web3 gaming with real blockchain mechanics, CDUST aims to build an active mining community while maintaining a sustainable and deflationary token economy.

With structured emissions, fair mining distribution, and transparent tokenomics, CDUST ensures a long-term balanced ecosystem.
