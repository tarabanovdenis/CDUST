Whitepaper

Abstract

CDUST is an innovative Web3 mining platform built on the Solana blockchain, allowing users to convert unused tokens (crypto dust) into mining power and earn CDUST. This document outlines the CDUST system, tokenomics, and game mechanics that create a unique, sustainable, and potentially profitable experience for cryptocurrency users.

Introduction

The evolution of blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies has opened new opportunities for gamification in the financial sector. CDUST leverages this potential by creating a virtual mining ecosystem on the Solana blockchain. Users can participate in cryptocurrency mining through the website without requiring physical mining equipment.

Registration and Purchasing Mining Power

Users join the CDUST ecosystem by registering on the official website. After registration, the system generates a unique mining wallet. The primary resource for mining is Hash Power (HP), which is acquired as follows:

  • Purchase the HP token on the Pump.fun platform via our website.
  • Send HP to the designated mining wallet; once transferred, the tokens are burned, and mining begins.

Block Generation and Mining Process

CDUST maintains a stable generation of 1,200 blocks daily:

  • Block processing time: 1-2 minutes, ensuring a steady mining pace.
  • Each mined block distributes CDUST among active miners proportionally to their Hash Power (HP).

Reward Distribution

  • CDUST rewards are distributed every hour.
  • Tokens are automatically sent to the user’s designated wallet.

Total Supply and Token Distribution

CDUST has a fixed total supply of 1,000,000,000 CDUST, allocated as follows:

  • 750,000,000 CDUST (75%) — allocated for mining rewards.
  • 150,000,000 CDUST (15%) — allocated for DEX and CEX liquidity.
  • 100,000,000 CDUST (10%) — allocated for community rewards and marketing.

Risks and Disclaimer

  • CDUST is a blockchain-based gaming platform designed for entertainment and engagement in the Web3 space.
  • The CDUST token is not an investment asset and should not be purchased with expectations of financial returns.
  • The cryptocurrency market is highly volatile, and participants risk losing their entire contribution.
  • The game mechanics and tokenomics described in this document are subject to change as the project evolves.
  • Participation in CDUST should be limited to funds users can afford to lose.
  • The project may face risks such as hacks, economic changes, or unforeseen challenges.
  • This document does not constitute financial, investment, or trading advice.

Conclusion

CDUST introduces a novel approach to virtual mining, leveraging the Solana blockchain to create an engaging and potentially profitable experience.

By integrating Web3 gaming with real blockchain mechanics, CDUST aims to build an active mining community while maintaining a sustainable and deflationary token economy. With structured emissions, fair mining distribution, and transparent tokenomics, CDUST ensures a long-term balanced ecosystem.
